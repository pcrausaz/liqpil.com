Pagoda

Beta

A digital implementation of Tichu, the classic four-player partnership trick-taking card game. Play online with friends or practice against AI opponents powered by a neural network trained on millions of human games. Features multiplayer lobbies with invite codes, ML and rule-based bots, two card deck styles, full Tichu rules, and player profiles with stats tracking.
